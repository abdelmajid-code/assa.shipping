About Us

A Moroccan Holding Company Built on Trust in Transport

ASSA SHIPPING operates as a single-shareholder limited liability company (SARL à associé unique) registered in Morocco, active in the transport sector under the classification "Activités des sociétés holding". Headquartered in the Agadir – Ida Ou Tanane region with commercial presence in Casablanca, we structure and support shipping and logistics operations for partners across Moroccan and international markets.

Our position as a holding entity within the transport sector allows us to coordinate freight, maritime and logistics interests with the discipline of a corporate structure and the responsiveness of a dedicated operations team.
